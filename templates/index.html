Did you know that batik originates from Indonesia?

In 2009, batik has officially become Indonesian cultural heritage and has been designated by the United Nations Educational, Scientific, and Cultural Organization (UNESCO). It is only natural that batik is inaugurated as Indonesia's cultural heritage. Batik itself is a craft that has high artistic value and has been part of Indonesian culture for a long time. If previously batik was only intended for kings, now Indonesian batik has developed into a work that is almost certain to exist in all regions of Indonesia and of course can be used by all groups of people.

Indonesian Batik

Batik is a craft made specifically on cloth that has high artistic value and has been part of Indonesian (especially Javanese) culture for a long time. Batik has a variety of motifs, each of which has a very deep philosophy. Batik is now a trend for various kinds of very beautiful products.
