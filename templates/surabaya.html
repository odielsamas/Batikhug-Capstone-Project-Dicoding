The city of Surabaya is also famous for its Batik House Tourism in Surabaya which is very interesting to visit. If you are traveling in Surabaya, stop by Rumah Batik. Rumah Batik has a diversity of ancestral heritage that has become an Indonesian icon. Batik is a very valuable traditional heritage and has become an identical icon from Indonesia. Each region has the characteristics of each batik and which symbolizes the characteristics of each ethnic group in Indonesia, which refers to diversity.
In this Surabaya batik house, there are collections of batik from seventeen districts which have their own varieties and patterns. The seventeen districts include Sumenep, Pamekasan, Sampang, Bangkalan, Surabaya, Sidoarjo, Lamongan, Tuban, Pasuruan, Malang, Batu, Jombang, Kediri, Pacitan, Banyuwangi, Jember and Tulungagung. There are more than 2000 pieces of batik cloth with different patterns and motifs.
