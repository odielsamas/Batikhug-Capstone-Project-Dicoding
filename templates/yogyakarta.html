Kampung Batik Giriloyo

Located in Yogyakarta, Central Java

Giriloyo batik tourism as a place to further introduce batik as one of Indonesia's most famous and iconic cultural heritages. However, many are still ignorant of this fact. Knowledge of batik is limited to local cloth products. In fact, apart from exploring its history, you can also enjoy the uniqueness of batik directly by participating in its production process in various batik tourism centers. Currently, there are batik tourism centers that not only show the beauty and uniqueness of batik as a very philosophical cultural product. This tour allows you to participate in the process of making batik.

Giriloyo batik tourism provides various destination packages. Here we describe the facilities that you can enjoy:
1. Batik Making Tour Packages
This package is available for those of you who want to be directly involved in making batik. The process includes depicting patterns, using canting, coloring, and making batik. You will be given a small piece of white cloth (mori) which you will make your own batik with.
After that, you are invited to process the cloth starting from soaking it with dyes, boiling, washing it, then drying it and airing it (mbabar process) to produce a small masterpiece in the form of finished "batik cloth". Your batik work can also be brought home as a memento.

2. Batik and Pilgrimage Tourism Education Packages
This package is available for those of you who want to travel. We will take you around the traditional houses and tombs of the kings of Jogja and Solo at the top of Imogiri Hill. This tour package will give you a thick historical and philosophical sensation.

3. Course Package
This course is for those of you who want to master the techniques of making batik from beginner to advanced levels. The implementation time is determined by mutual agreement, what material the prospective participants want to learn, then it will be agreed on the implementation time. This training includes theory and practice accompanied by an experienced Teaching Team.
